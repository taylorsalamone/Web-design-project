My project pitch draft!

At first, I had a bit of trouble coming up with an idea for my project pitch.
But then I got to thinking...why don't I create a social media campaign for something that I am passionate about?
The answer?

Boneless. Wings.

As of late there has been a fiery debate about whether boneless wings are chicken nuggets.
As an avid boneless wing fan, I am proposing we settle this argument once and for all by declaring through a cohesive campaign that...

boneless wings ARE NOT chicken nuggets!!!!

Why boneless wings?

Well, let me tell you:

  • Boneless wings are fundamentally different than nugs and the world needs to see that!
  • I personally love b-less wings and prefer them to ordinary wings
  • I think creating a social media campaign for a lighthearted topic will be a fun experience

What would the social media campaign consist of?

  • Facebook and Twitter cover photos and profile pics
  • Ads/graphics promoting our stance for social media timelines
  • Website to act as the hub for the campaign (people can go there for more detailed information)
  • Short YouTube promotional video
So, if you think we are two birds of a feather then
JOIN ME in educating the world about chicken!
